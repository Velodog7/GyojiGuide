Health watch · Nagoya 2026

  • Onosato (Y) — left shoulder since Nov; sat out all of May, surgery was floated. Says he’s “definitely in,” but durability is the storyline.
  • Hoshoryu (Y) — pulled out Day 1 of May with a right hamstring; reports he’s managing it.
  • Aonishiki (S) — broken toe / left foot from early-May practice; missed May and dropped from Ozeki. Regains Ozeki with 10 wins.
  • Kotozakura (O) — withdrew Day 11 of May; kadoban, needs 8 wins to hold the rank.
  • Takayasu (M7) — withdrew injured during May; fitness for Nagoya is the question.
  • Asanoyama (M10) — withdrew in May with a Lisfranc injury to the left foot.
